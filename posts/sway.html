Switching to Sway


You heard me. I have officially switched to SwayWM from Hyprland. I'll tell why in just a moment, but first let's just go through my "Sway Journey"

Installing it was simple, just install sway?! And then you remove hyprland which in turn removes xwayland alongside it, so might want to install xwayland again.

Configuring Sway is not hard, it has a simple configuration similar to that of i3, which is to be expected, since it is specifically i3 compatible. I just used what I always used, waybar, rofi etc, as well as a colorscheme i've never tried out, everforest, it's nice!

What I think, is the philosophy of Sway

SwayWM, despite being a "Wayland Compositor" does not have any eye candy, I love this. I know there are projects like swayFX but the whole point of sway, to me atleast is that it is incredibly, not distracting!
Just think about it, animations make everything slower, rounded corners waste battery life (who cares but I thought i'd mention it, still not a fan of rounded corners), and blur is just.. eye candy, nothing else.
The philosophy of a window manager itself is to be faster than a desktop environment and get your things done without touching the mouse. i3 is a perfect example of a window manager, it has no bloat, it's just fast, get's my stuff done and that's it. When back then, i used i3, I did have blur, shadows, rounded corners, etc, with the reason being it's pretty cool to see all of that in a X11 window manager, where you have to get 

 picom
and configure it, i will never get this feeling with hyprland.

Reasons for me to switch to sway

  • It is simply more minimal
  • I'd like a wlroots WM
  • It is, somehow, easier to configure
  • THIS (sway) is what i've always tried to get in Hyprland, but then forced to enable a lot of the eyecandy, cause if they are there, I wanna use em, If not, good for me.
  • I thought i'd try something new
  • https://rentry.co/Hyprland-controversy


Problems with Sway

It ain't all sunshine and rainbows of course, the number one problem I had with sway was OBS and screen sharing.
unlike hyprland, just installing these packages won't work: 
xdg-desktop-portal xdg-desktop-portal-wlr*
*Note, xdg-desktop-portal-wlr as the name suggests, is for wlroots WMs, hyprland uses xdg-desktop-portal-hyprland

The one additional step is just adding these commands to autostart like so,

In ~/.config/sway/config: 
exec --no-startup-id /usr/lib/xdg-desktop-portal
exec --no-startup-id /usr/lib/xdg-desktop-portal-wlr


And there's that. No other problems i face with sway, the ones i did face i fixed.

Conclusion

Sway is just better than Hyprland for me, I do not want to get into a lot of the drama with hyprland, and originally this was a rage decision after getting a banned from the hyprland discord and then "Going back after a day or so", but it's discord who cares.
